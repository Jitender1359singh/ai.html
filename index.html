Human-robots

The term "human-robot collaboration" refers to a type of robotics where humans and robots work together to complete tasks or achieve a common goal. This concept is also known as co-robotics or robolution. Human-robot collaboration enables humans to focus on tasks that require creativity, decision-making, and high-level skills, while robots handle repetitive, precision-based tasks or those that require heavy lifting.
